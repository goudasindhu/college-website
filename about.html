About Our College

SJR College of Science, Arts and Commerce is one of the most reputed educational institutions in Bangalore. Established with the aim of providing quality higher education, the college focuses on academic excellence, discipline, and all-round development of students.

Our Vision

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values that will help them build a successful future and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

  • To provide high-quality education
  • To encourage creativity and critical thinking
  • To build confidence and leadership qualities in students
  • To promote cultural, academic, and sports excellence

Facilities

  • Well-equipped computer and science labs
  • Experienced teaching faculty
  • Playground and cultural activity space
  • Hostel facility for boys and girls
